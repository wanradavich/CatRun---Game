Welcome to

Cat Run!

Once upon a time, in a colorful Cat World, there lived a friendly cat who loved to run. It loved to explore and eat delicious fish. One day, the world rained fish and filled the grounds with the tastiest fish ever! But to enjoy good and tasty fish, it needs your help.

Eat Good Fish:

Cat is very hungry! Help it move around the Cat World using the arrow keys on your keyboard. Guide him to eat only the good fish that appear to score 1 point each time. Every fish it eats makes it grow 1 bit longer and stronger!

Eat Magical Fish:

Look out for the magical fish! They move around very quickly, but once you catch one you're in luck! Each of these fish contain a random score (1-6 points) that help increase your points and 1 bit body length.

Avoid Bad Fish:

Beware of bad fish traps. At first glance, they look just like a good fish. But each bad fish will decrease your score by 20 points and increasing a random number of body bits to your cat. If your score is less than 0, it's gameover. Also, don't let your cat run into walls or itself!
