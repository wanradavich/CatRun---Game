Welcome to

Cat Run!

...Once upon a time, in a colorful Cat World, there lived a friendly cat who loved to run. It loved to explore and eat delicious fish. One day, the world rained fish and filled the grounds with the tastiest fish ever! But to enjoy good and tasty fish, it needs your help.

Eat Good Fish:

Cat is very hungry! Help it move around the Cat World using the arrow keys on your keyboard. Guide him to eat only good and magical fish that appear to score a point. Every fish it eats makes it grow longer and stronger!

Eat Magical Fish:

Cat is keeping score of how much it eats. It's ultimate dream is to brag to the cats back home! Help the cat score more points by eating Magical Fish. You never know how many points Magical Fish might bring you.

Avoid Bad Fish:

Beware of tricky bad fish traps! At first glimpse, they look just like a good fish. Aside from that, Don't let Cat run into the walls or himself. If he does, the game will be over.
